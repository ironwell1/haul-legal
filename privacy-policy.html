Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 1, 2026

Caloriv ("the app") is operated by Jackson Rohrs ("I", "me", or "my"). This Privacy Policy explains how I collect, use, and protect your information when you use Caloriv on your iOS device.

Information I Collect

Caloriv is designed to work primarily on your device. Here is what the app collects and stores:

  • Profile data — age, height, weight, fitness goal, and macro targets you enter during onboarding. This is stored locally on your device using AsyncStorage.
  • Meal log data — voice transcripts and the food entries you log each day, including their nutritional information. Stored locally on your device.
  • Voice transcripts — when you use voice logging, your speech is transcribed on-device using Apple's built-in speech recognition. The resulting text description of your meal is sent to the Anthropic Claude API to calculate macros. No audio is ever transmitted.
  • Meal parsing — your text description of a meal is sent to the Anthropic Claude API to identify foods and estimate calories and macros. No personally identifiable information such as your name or location is included in these requests.
  • Purchase information — if you subscribe, payment is processed by Apple. I use RevenueCat to manage subscription status. RevenueCat receives a randomized identifier and subscription status — not your name, email, or payment details.

Information I Do Not Collect

  • I do not collect your name, email address, or any contact information unless you contact me directly.
  • I do not collect location data.
  • I do not record or store audio from your voice logs.
  • I do not use advertising SDKs or sell your data to third parties.
  • I do not track you across other apps or websites.
  • I do not have access to your payment information — all payments are handled by Apple.

Third-Party Services

Caloriv uses the following third-party services to function:

  • Apple Speech Recognition — on-device transcription of your voice input. No audio leaves your device. Apple's privacy policy is at apple.com/privacy.
  • Anthropic Claude API — used to calculate calories and macros from your meal descriptions. Requests include your text description of a meal but not personally identifiable information. Anthropic's privacy policy is at anthropic.com/privacy.
  • RevenueCat — used to manage subscription status. RevenueCat's privacy policy is at revenuecat.com/privacy.

Data Storage and Security

All your meal logs, profile data, and streak history are stored locally on your device and never transmitted to my servers. Data sent to third-party APIs (meal parsing) is used only to fulfill your request and is not stored by me.

I take reasonable measures to protect your information but no method of electronic storage or transmission is 100% secure.

Children's Privacy

Caloriv is not directed at children under the age of 13. I do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information, please contact me and I will delete it promptly.

Your Rights

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you can delete it at any time by going to Profile → Clear all meal logs within the app, or by uninstalling Caloriv.

To request deletion of any data associated with your account, contact me at helphaulapp@outlook.com.
