Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 21, 2026

Haul ("the app") is operated by Jackson Rohrs ("I", "me", or "my"). This Privacy Policy explains how I collect, use, and protect your information when you use Haul on your iOS device.

Information I Collect

Haul is designed to work primarily on your device. Here is what the app collects and stores:

  • Profile data — age, height, weight, fitness goal, and dietary preferences you enter during onboarding. This is stored locally on your device using AsyncStorage.
  • Pantry data — food items you scan or search, including their nutritional information. Stored locally on your device.
  • Meal plan data — the AI-generated meal plans created for you. Stored locally on your device.
  • Barcode scans — when you scan a barcode, the barcode number is sent to Open Food Facts (openfoodfacts.org) and the USDA FoodData Central API to retrieve nutritional information. No personal information is sent with these requests.
  • Meal plan generation — your pantry contents and profile (goal, macros, meal preferences) are sent to a Supabase Edge Function which calls the Anthropic Claude API to generate your meal plan. No personally identifiable information such as your name or email is included in these requests.
  • Purchase information — if you subscribe, payment is processed by Apple. I use RevenueCat to manage subscription status. RevenueCat receives a randomized identifier and subscription status — not your name, email, or payment details.

Information I Do Not Collect

  • I do not collect your name, email address, or any contact information unless you contact me directly.
  • I do not collect location data.
  • I do not use advertising SDKs or sell your data to third parties.
  • I do not track you across other apps or websites.
  • I do not have access to your payment information — all payments are handled by Apple.

Third-Party Services

Haul uses the following third-party services to function:

  • Open Food Facts — an open-source food database used for barcode lookups. Privacy policy at openfoodfacts.org/privacy.
  • USDA FoodData Central — a public food database operated by the U.S. Department of Agriculture.
  • Anthropic Claude API — used to generate personalized meal plans. Requests include your pantry and profile data but not personally identifiable information. Anthropic's privacy policy is at anthropic.com/privacy.
  • Supabase — used to host the meal plan generation function. Supabase's privacy policy is at supabase.com/privacy.
  • RevenueCat — used to manage subscription status. RevenueCat's privacy policy is at revenuecat.com/privacy.

Data Storage and Security

Most of your data is stored locally on your device and never transmitted to my servers. Data sent to third-party APIs (barcode lookups and meal plan generation) is used only to fulfill your request and is not stored by me.

I take reasonable measures to protect your information but no method of electronic storage or transmission is 100% secure.

Children's Privacy

Haul is not directed at children under the age of 13. I do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information, please contact me and I will delete it promptly.

Your Rights

Since most data is stored locally on your device, you can delete it at any time by going to Settings → Reset all data within the app, or by uninstalling Haul.

To request deletion of any data associated with your account, contact me at helphaulapp@outlook.com.
